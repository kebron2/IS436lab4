Welcome to Department of Information Systems

Welcome to the Department of Information Systems at UMBC. Our interdisciplinary program helps students develop in-demand skills, preparing them for careers in IT and IS. Located near technology hubs, our suburban campus is an exciting place for students in tech. With a variety of degree programs to choose from, we offer programs designed to give you options for applying information systems to your career. To learn more, please contact us with any questions.

About

The Department of Information Systems is a key part of the College of Engineering and Information Technology (COEIT) at UMBC. We offer a diverse range of undergraduate and graduate degrees and certificates, including a B.A. in Business Technology Administration, a B.S. in Information Systems, combined B.S./M.S. programs, an M.S. in Information Systems (available online), an M.S. in Human-Centered Computing, and Ph.D. programs in Information Systems and Human-Centered Computing.
