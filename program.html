Undergraduate Programs

UMBC is ranked a Top 10 Most Innovative School by U.S. News and World Report. The Information Systems Department continues that proud tradition in our undergraduate programs and courses. We offer a variety of majors, minors, and certificates to help you reach your potential.

  • Bachelor’s Degree Programs

    Our undergraduate degree programs provide a solid foundation in Information Systems. These programs are designed to prepare students for a career in the rapidly growing IT industry.

  • Accelerated Programs – Combined Bachelor’s and Master’s

    UMBC offers an accelerated program that allows students to complete both a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Information Systems in a shorter time frame, enhancing career opportunities.

  • Undergraduate Certificate Programs

    Students can also opt for specialized undergraduate certificates, which provide advanced knowledge and skills in specific areas of Information Systems.

  • Undergraduate Course Listing and Internships

    The Information Systems Department offers a comprehensive listing of courses, and students can gain hands-on experience through internships with leading companies in the tech industry.

  • Undergraduate Advising

    Our academic advisors are here to guide you through your academic journey, helping you choose the right courses and career path.

  • Undergraduate Research Opportunities

    We offer research opportunities for undergraduate students to engage in cutting-edge projects and gain valuable experience in Information Systems research.
